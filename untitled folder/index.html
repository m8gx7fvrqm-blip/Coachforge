Is this really affordable?

+

Yes! Start at just $47/month with a 7-day free trial. Many members reduce their cost to $20-30/month through our referral and milestone programs. We're committed to making coaching accessible.

How long until I'm certified?

+

Most members complete certification in 5-6 months. The timeline depends on your pace and commitment. You can accelerate by being more active in the community and completing milestones faster.

Can I really earn money while learning?

+

Absolutely! Mentors earn $50/month per mentee. Refer clients and earn 15% commission. Refer new members and get monthly discounts. Many active members offset their entire subscription cost.

What if I just want coaching, not to become a coach?

+

That's perfectly fine! Many members stay in the Foundation Phase and just receive coaching. You're never pressured to become a coach. You can stay at the Starter tier and get great value.

How does the referral discount work?

+

Refer a friend: You get $10/month off permanently. They get 20% off their first 3 months. Refer 3 friends and your subscription could be free! There's no limit to referrals.

Is there a contract or commitment?

+

No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime. We're confident you'll love it, so we make it easy to leave - but most members stay because the value keeps growing.
